Air Pollution - Notes

Air pollution is the presence of harmful substances in the air, often resulting from human activities such as industrial production, vehicle emissions, and waste burning. It has adverse effects on human health, the environment, and contributes to global warming.

  • Major pollutants include carbon monoxide, sulfur dioxide, nitrogen oxides, and particulate matter.
  • Health effects can include respiratory issues, cardiovascular diseases, and aggravation of existing health conditions.
  • Prevention includes using cleaner energy sources, reducing vehicle emissions, and promoting public transportation.
